Services we offer

Property management and agency

We manage properties on behalf of landlords by advertising and filling vacancies; negotiating and preparing leases. We also manage properties on behalf of landlords by; collecting rent, maintaining premises, attending to faults and other tenant complaints.

Property valuations

We do market assessments for various purposes to ascertain approximate values for properties; market value, forced sale value, rental value, insurance value and valuation for collateral purposes. We also handle valuations for various types of properties, that ranges from fixed assets (Real Estate) to movables (Machinery and chattels).

Processing Titles

We handle the processing involved in legally transferring the titles of property from sellers(vendors) to a buyers (purchasers), including undertaking a variety of searches to confirm property ownership, and the preparation and execution of a number of legal documents associated with the transactions.

Property Development

We work with Developers in the search for suitable land opportunities and carry out the feasibility studies of their desired projects. We also Plan the different stages of the development including schedules and costs by coordinating with architects, engineers, quantity surveyors and other consultants.

Provision of house plans

Clients who wish to construct can also choose from our wide range of house plans available to them.
